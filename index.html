College Notes Uploading System (Web Application)

The College Notes Uploading System is designed to facilitate the seamless upload, download, and management of course-specific notes for users. With a user-friendly interface, the system prioritizes simplicity to ensure easy navigation and accessibility. Administrators hold the authority to oversee and manage users, access their uploads, and delete content as needed. Users can log in to gain access to a diverse range of course notes, enhancing the overall experience of learning and collaboration within the platform. The system's core objective is to provide a straightforward and efficient solution for students and administrators alike in managing and accessing educational resources.

Student Transportation and Notification System (IOT)

The integration of RFID technology, GPS, and a microcontroller empowers real-time school bus tracking, ensuring enhanced safety measures. RFID tags uniquely identify students, while GPS technology tracks the bus's precise location. The accompanying application enables parents to conveniently track the bus, access pickup and drop-off times, and stay informed about their child's safety throughout the journey. Additionally, bus drivers utilize switches for instant departure updates and can submit bus-related complaint reports through the app, collectively contributing to the overall well-being and security of the students.
